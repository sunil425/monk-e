Our belief

MA true cannabis was established in 2018 with the aim of profoundly redesigning the cannabis market at an international level. Based in Switzerland, we proudly produce the best Cannabis Flowers in the world, in full compliance with the limits imposed by law.

MA true cannabis is produced indoors in an eco-friendly, innovative and organic way. MA true cannabis is a brand that actively respects and encourages Corporate Social

Responsibility: valuing our employees, happy work environment, employee empowerment, strict control of supply chains preventing child labour are just a few examples of our way of conduction business.
